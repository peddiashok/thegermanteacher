01 — About Me

The person behind the lesson

I'm Ashok Peddi — a language trainer with a simple belief: every person has an extraordinary capacity to communicate, connect, and grow. My job is to help them find it.

I came to teaching through my own obsession with learning. I know the frustration of a grammar rule that won't stick, the thrill of your first real conversation, and the patience it takes to get there. That lived experience shapes every class I run.

Whether you're picking up your very first German words or polishing your English for professional life — I meet you exactly where you are and walk forward with you, one step at a time. No shortcuts. No fluff. Just progress.

"Languages aren't just grammar rules and vocabulary lists. They are living, breathing systems of human thought — and I help you think in them."

🎯
Specialisation
German A1 to B1 using the Netzwerk framework, and English communication for students & professionals.
📚
Teaching Method
Context-bound learning, communicative tasks, and structured grammar — with real-life relevance at every step.
🌐
Languages
German · English · Telugu · Hindi — a multilingual background means I understand the learner's struggle from the inside.
📅
Availability
Weekend intensive batches (Saturday, 6 hrs) and weekday 1-on-1 slots. Online & in-person (Indore).
🏆
Exam Preparation
Goethe-Zertifikat A1, A2, B1 and TELC equivalents — integrated into curriculum with mock tests & strategy.

04 — German Level Roadmap

Your journey from A1 to B1

A1
Beginner
A1 — Breakthrough
~80–100 hrs · 2 sub-levels

Greetings, introductions, numbers, basic grammar, daily vocabulary. Your entire foundation is built here.

A2
Elementary
A2 — Waystage
~100–150 hrs · 2 sub-levels

Describe your world — family, work, shopping, directions. Simple past, modal verbs, prepositions mastered.

B1
Intermediate
B1 — Threshold
~150–200 hrs · 2 sub-levels

Handle travel, work, and social situations confidently. Complex sentences, Perfekt, Konjunktiv II.

06 — FAQ

Common questions

Not at all! The A1 course starts from absolute zero — the German alphabet, basic pronunciation, and your very first greetings. All you need is the motivation to learn.

I'm Ashok Peddi — a language trainer with a simple belief: every person has an extraordinary capacity to communicate, connect, and grow. My job is to help them find it.

I came to teaching through my own obsession with learning. I know the frustration of a grammar rule that won't stick, the thrill of your first real conversation, and the patience it takes to get there. That lived experience shapes every class I run.

Whether you're picking up your very first German words or polishing your English for professional life — I meet you exactly where you are and walk forward with you, one step at a time. No shortcuts. No fluff. Just progress.

"Languages aren't just grammar rules and vocabulary lists. They are living, breathing systems of human thought — and I help you think in them."

04 — German Level Roadmap

Your journey from A1 to B1

A1
Beginner
A1 — Breakthrough
~80–100 hrs · 2 sub-levels

Greetings, introductions, numbers, basic grammar, daily vocabulary. Your entire foundation is built here.

A2
Elementary
A2 — Waystage
~100–150 hrs · 2 sub-levels

Describe your world — family, work, shopping, directions. Simple past, modal verbs, prepositions mastered.

B1
Intermediate
B1 — Threshold
~150–200 hrs · 2 sub-levels

Handle travel, work, and social situations confidently. Complex sentences, Perfekt, Konjunktiv II.
